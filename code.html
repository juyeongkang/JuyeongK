Get my Identity, 2021



“Get my Identity,” the imaginary being will tell your randomized Korean name and its meaning. The very first page with abstract symbolized texts contains a description of Korean national culture. I got inspired by the movie Matrix's first scene when Trinity talks through the computer to Neo, saying, "Hi, Neo.” People sometimes desire to be someone else. For instance, people are living with different identities online.
